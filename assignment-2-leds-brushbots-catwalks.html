Assignment 2
LEDs, Brushbots and Catwalks

Get your 4 or more Resistors ( specific Values TBA) and your LEDs and set up on your breadboard 4 serial circuits ( with at least 3 leds). You are take the 4 serial strads and make parallel circuits on your breadboard.

Back to Portfolio Home

Project Description

Create 2 solid wire-tipped leads. Each one should be 2-inch solid 3-inch braided and 2-inch solid. So solid on ends and braided in the middle. Each lead will need to be about 7 inches long. Ideally, the solid color for each cable should be different ( black and red tipped) but the braided can be the same color. These solid wires will used to plug into the bread board. Details and examples will be provided in class.
